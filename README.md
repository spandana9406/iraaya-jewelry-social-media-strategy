# Social Media Content Strategy for Iraaya Jewelry

## Project Overview

This capstone project focuses on developing a social media content strategy for Iraaya Jewelry, a handmade jewelry brand specializing in earrings, bracelets, neck chains, and customized accessories.

The project aims to improve brand awareness, audience engagement, and customer acquisition through strategic Instagram marketing.

---

## Objectives

- Increase brand awareness
- Improve customer engagement
- Build a consistent brand identity
- Generate product inquiries and sales
- Develop a structured content strategy

---

## Target Audience

- Age Group: 16–35 years
- Primarily female customers
- Students and working professionals
- Fashion and accessory enthusiasts

---

## Content Strategy

### Content Pillars

1. Product Showcase
2. Behind-the-Scenes Content
3. Educational Content
4. Customer Testimonials
5. Engagement Content

---

## Tools Used

- Canva
- Instagram Insights
- Meta Business Suite
- Google Analytics

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Reach
- Impressions
- Likes
- Comments
- Shares
- Saves
- Product Inquiries
- Sales Conversions

---

## Project Outcome

The proposed strategy helps Iraaya Jewelry establish a stronger digital presence, increase audience engagement, and create more opportunities for customer acquisition and sales growth.
